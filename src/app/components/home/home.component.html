Sofia Carrasco is a tenor saxophonist and visual artist from Arequipa, Perú. Honesty in interpretation is Sofia’s core value. Currently, she is creating a project called Afro – Andean Jazz by Sofia where her original artwork will accompany the musical experience of mixing the language of jazz harmony and improvisation with the rooted characteristic of Andean and African rhythms.
